A Perpetual Motion Machine (The Magnetic Force Does No Work on Moving Charges)

This idea was not originally mine. I can't find the physics blogger that inspired me.
​
​Yes, I realize the colours are ugly. That isn't what's interesting. What is really cool about this simulation is that because the magnetic force can't do work on moving charges, the rotational energy can be converted to translational kinetic energy. This process is cyclical as the kinetic energy is converted back into rotational energy.

But why does this happen and what is going on?

​The spheres on the ring are four positive charges. You can think of them as protons. The arrows represents the external magnetic field acting on the whole system. When the ring is given some initial spin a dipole is created. Since the magnetic fields point in the same direction they attract each other. Torque is then applied so the ring spins in the opposite direction and the disk is repelled.
